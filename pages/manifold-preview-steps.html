Notes on the design

  • All algorithm color accents removed. Three dot states: filled white (past or current), hollow white (future), dim gray (N/A for this algorithm).
  • Both bars render all seven canonical positions 0..6, so the bars stay vertically aligned across both panels. N/A positions sit in their canonical column with a dim gray dot and a faded number label.
  • Edges and dots are vertically centered on the same line. Number labels float below each dot via absolute positioning so they do not push the edge off the dot center.
  • Edges remain the click target for expand or collapse. Dots remain the click target for jump-to-step. N/A dots are non-interactive.
  • In the expanded list, the current row gets a subtle background tint and bold weight. N/A rows are italic and faded with a dim gray marker.
