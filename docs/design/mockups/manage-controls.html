Grouped grid

Labeled groups, all visible at once, organized by purpose. Best when every control is used often. Can get wide.

B

Primary + "more"

Most-used controls visible; secondary behind a disclosure. Keeps the default view calm; advanced is one click away.

C

Tabbed groups

One group at a time via tabs. Smallest footprint; but hides controls and adds a click to reach them.
