A

Minimal / underline

Text-like controls, underline affordances. Most editorial and airy. Risk: smaller hit areas, affordance can be subtle on touch.

B

Quiet filled (recommended)

Subtle filled surfaces + hairline borders, rounded. Larger tap targets, clearer "this is interactive", still minimal and dark.

C

Mix

e.g. filled selects/inputs/buttons (B) but keep underline tabs and text-style primary (A). Tell me the combo.
