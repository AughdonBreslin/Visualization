1

No frames

Everything flush; only hairline rules + whitespace. Purest editorial. Risk: live plot/controls can feel like they float without an edge.

2

Frame the instruments (recommended)

Prose stays unboxed; only interactive figures + controls get a quiet hairline frame. Keeps reading airy, gives the "live" area a clear edge.

3

Quiet containers

Every section sits on a faint surface. Most structured, least airy. Closest to the current site, just refined.
