A

Neutral graphite

True grays, no temperature. Cleanest and most timeless; lets the accent be the only color. Closest to today.

B

Cool slate

Faint blue cast in neutrals. Reads modern/technical and "screen-native". Pairs naturally with a blue accent.

C

Warm carbon

Faint warm cast. Softer, more editorial/premium (the 23street feel). Pairs better with a warm or neutral accent.
